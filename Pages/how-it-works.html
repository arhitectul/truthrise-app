Safety Tips

Essential guidelines to keep you safe while reporting fraud.

Never Share Sensitive Credentials

Never share bank logins, full ID scans, or passwords publicly. Keep sensitive information private.

Use Safe Documentation

Use screenshots, receipts, and safe document uploads. Redact personal information when possible.

Stay on Platform

Keep communication on the TruthRise platform to avoid further scams and maintain security.
