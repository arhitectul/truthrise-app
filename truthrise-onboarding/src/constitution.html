TRUTHRISE Constitution

Welcome to TRUTHRISE!

Please read and acknowledge each article of our Constitution. You must confirm each article before proceeding.

Article 1 of 3 ▼
Transparency
We commit to full transparency in all operations, communications, and decision-making processes.
  • 1.1 All actions are documented and accessible to the community.
  • 1.2 Financial flows are published and auditable.
  • 1.3 Decisions are made in public forums whenever possible.
Article 2 of 3 ▼
Respect
We foster a respectful and inclusive environment for all members.
  • 2.1 Discrimination or harassment is not tolerated.
  • 2.2 Constructive feedback is encouraged.
  • 2.3 Diversity of thought is valued.
Article 3 of 3 ▼
Integrity
We act with honesty and uphold the highest ethical standards.
  • 3.1 Truthfulness is expected in all interactions.
  • 3.2 Conflicts of interest must be disclosed.
  • 3.3 Misrepresentation is grounds for removal.
