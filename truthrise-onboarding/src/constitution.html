TRUTHRISE Constitution

Welcome to TRUTHRISE!

Please read and acknowledge each article of our Constitution. You must confirm each article before proceeding.

Article 1 of 8 ▼
Article I: Mission of Protection
TruthRise shields users from deception, aids recovery, and equips individuals to reclaim control over their digital lives. We are a safe haven for those navigating the risks of online environments.
  • 1.1 TruthRise shields users from deception.
  • 1.2 Aids recovery.
  • 1.3 Equips individuals to reclaim control over their digital lives.
Article 2 of 8 ▼
Article II: Ethics Above All
We pledge never to exploit those seeking help. Our commitment: no misleading claims, no inflated fees, no pressure. Transparency, care, and integrity guide every interaction.
  • 2.1 No misleading claims.
  • 2.2 No inflated fees.
  • 2.3 No pressure.
Article 3 of 8 ▼
Article III: People Before Platforms
TruthRise prioritizes the vulnerable: those manipulated by unregulated markets, deceptive advisors, or complex systems. Our loyalty lies with users who trust us with their stories and pursuit of justice.
  • 3.1 Prioritizes the vulnerable.
  • 3.2 Loyalty lies with users.
  • 3.3 Trust us with their stories.
Article 4 of 8 ▼
Article IV: Fair, Transparent Pricing
Protection must be accessible. TruthRise services are clearly priced, fairly structured, and openly communicated. No hidden fees; no traps. The cost of action will never cause harm.
  • 4.1 Clearly priced services.
  • 4.2 Fairly structured.
  • 4.3 No hidden fees.
Article 5 of 8 ▼
Article V: Collaboration, Not Control
TruthRise is a participatory system, not a hierarchy. Contributors, validators, whistleblowers, and experts shape our work. Decisions arise from community dialogue, with power shared, not hoarded.
  • 5.1 Participatory system.
  • 5.2 Community dialogue.
  • 5.3 Power shared.
Article 6 of 8 ▼
Article VI: Accountability for All
From founders to partners, everyone is accountable. No one is above our standards of truth, integrity, and conduct. Violations trigger immediate investigation and public response.
  • 6.1 Everyone is accountable.
  • 6.2 Standards of truth.
  • 6.3 Immediate investigation.
Article 7 of 8 ▼
Article VII: Digital Dignity for Everyone
Protection is a right, not a privilege. Whether a pensioner, student, or first-time investor, every user deserves respect. We simplify complexity and make security accessible.
  • 7.1 Protection is a right.
  • 7.2 Every user deserves respect.
  • 7.3 Security accessible.
Article 8 of 8 ▼
Article VIII: Open Justice as Our Strength
TruthRise operates in the light. We expose fraud, trace networks, and document abuse. When justice is served, it is visible, driven by the collective power of the people.
  • 8.1 Expose fraud.
  • 8.2 Trace networks.
  • 8.3 Document abuse.
